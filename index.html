Our story

Where the trumpet breathes
and the coffee sings

A small, unhurried place in Surco. We believe the best conversations happen over an honest espresso, in a room where someone is playing Autumn Leaves just loud enough to feel it in your chest.

We are not a chain. We are not a concept. We are two friends, a secondhand record player, and an obsession with good beans. Come as you are.
